Roma Segreta

Three hours in the Rome no tour bus finds: a hundred cats guarding Caesar's murder scene, a frescoed courtyard office workers walk through blind, a drowned city under the Trevi — and a gelato dare to close it out.

The loop

One tight walking loop in the centro storico — about 2 km on foot, no rides between stops. Core loop is 2½ hours (14:30–17:10); at 17:15 it forks — bone crypt, or the shopping run (Shop tab), or both if legs allow. Tap a stop for the story; tick it off as you go.

What got cut

The full-day version had the Aventine keyhole, Rome's pyramid, the Testaccio market and the fairy-tale Coppedè district — all listed under Tips as "for another visit." This loop keeps the four things nobody back home will believe.

The shopping run

No souvenir stands, no "I ♥ Roma" shirts. A 273-year-old potion shop at the start of the loop, and a 17:15 fork instead of (or after) the crypt: a marble workshop north, teen vintage south. Both are a 12–15 minute walk from the gelato.
